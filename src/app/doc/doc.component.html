Home

On the front page, you can see all the unicorns of your herd.

Each unicorn is displayed in a card with the following informations :

  • A picture with their color
  • Their name
  • Their gender
  • Their age
  • A profile button (hover or tap)
  • A delete button (hover or tap)

The age is not in years but in rainbows (1 rainbow = 2 years).

Profile

On the profile page, you can see all the informations about a unicorn. Furthermore, if the unicorn has parents in the herd, they are displayed at the bottom.

Mating

In order to mate, a unicorn must be at least 9 rainbows (18 years old).

Only a male and a female can have a child. Each unicorn can only have one child. If a child is deleted, parents can have another child.
